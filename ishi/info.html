Eric is a Texas-born photographer and director based in Brooklyn. After a stint in the business world, he moved to New York, quit his day job and started making images full time.

Ten years later he has worked with some of the most notable talent in the world, directed national campaigns and commercials and grown his family from one to three.

He likes to shoot in a loose, fluid manner and tries to sneak at least one pack of polaroid into every shoot day. Based in New York with his wife and daughter, but they're totally good with him being on the road.
