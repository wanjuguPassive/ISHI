Ishi

‘Ishi’ is a Swahili word meaning ‘Live’. Live where your heart is. Live where your Art is.
We hope to inspire others to live doing what they truly love and are passionate about (where your heart is) as well as what they are gifted in (where your Art is).

As for us, the Visual-Digital Narrative did it. Here’s to those who love to create, and to do so boldly. For those who dare to live through their Art.
